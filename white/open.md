# Open Game 

## 1. e4 e5

White will play `2. Nf3`, developing a piece and attacking the pawn on `e5`.

### 2. Nf3 Nc6

Black moves his knight to `c6` to defend the pawn and develop a piece. White
plays `3. Bc4`, developing a piece and preparing to castle.
